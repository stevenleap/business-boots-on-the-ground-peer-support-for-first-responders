Field Notes

Confidential, caring, compassionate

Boots on the Ground Peer Support for First Responders is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing confidential, caring, and compassionate peer support to first responders across the province of Ontario. Our helpline operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that those who serve our communities have a safe, anonymous space to reach out for support.

  • ✓ Confidential and anonymous helpline
  • ✓ Staffed by trained peer supporters who understand first responder culture
  • ✓ Available 24/7 across Ontario
  • ✓ No cost to callers
